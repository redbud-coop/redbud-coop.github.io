Housing

Room Types

Single

  • There are nineteen singles in Redbud
  • Living in a single costs $2,550 per semester
  • Singles have queen beds
  • Singles are generally reserved for existing house members, but exceptions can be made

Double

  • There are six doubles in Redbud
  • Living in a double costs $2,225 per semester
  • Doubles have twin beds

Triple

  • There is one triple in Redbud
  • Living in a triple costs $1,910 per semester
  • The triple has twin beds

Meal Plan

The meal plan is $550 per semester. It includes dinner 6 days a week (Sunday-Friday) and staples. There is usually enough food for breakfast and lunch. As outlined in rules, each house memeber must be part of a cook group for one day a week where they cook for the rest of the house. There is meat at least 2 days a week and there is always a vegetarian option. We can accommodate any food allergies or dietary restrcitons, just let us know!

Example weekly menu

  • Sunday - Vegetarian Moroccan Stew
  • Monday - Lemon Herb Chicken with Coconut Rice and Potato Salad
  • Tuesday - Mushroom Spaghetti with Cheesy Brussel Sprouts
  • Wednesday - Sushi Bowls
  • Thursday - Green Curry Chicken Pasta with Thai Tea and Double Roasted Brussel Sprouts
  • Friday - Miso Soup with Pineapple Fried Rice

If you are joining us for a meal please add yourself to our Save Plate, with any allergies or dietary restrictions.
